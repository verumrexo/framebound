A space exploration roguelike built with vanilla JS and Vite.

## Features
- **Physics-based Flight**: Drift, boost, and navigate through asteroid fields.
- **Ship Building**: Collect parts and customize your ship's layout.
- **Economy**: Key/Crate logic, Gold, and Shop system.
- **Cursed Vaults**: Risk your life for high-tier loot in challenging ambush rooms.

## How to Play
[Play Online Here](https://verumrexo.github.io/framebound/) (GitHub Pages)

### Controls
- **WASD**: Move
- **Mouse**: Aim
- **Left Click**: Shoot
- **Shift**: Boost / Dash
- **E**: Interact / Open Chests
- **Tab**: Open Hangar (Inventory)
